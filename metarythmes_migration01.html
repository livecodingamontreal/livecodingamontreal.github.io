Metarythmes - migration 01 à OBORO, Montréal.







'migration 01' was the first jam/meeting of Metarythmes, it took place at OBORO in Montréal, and we were supported by STUDIO XX, OBORO and TOPLAP. The meeting was sui generis bringing together live improv unexpected materialities by media artists from diverse backgrounds and non conventional approaches.

Participants included:

Stephanie Castonguay, Charles Bicari (Vieux Néant), Marije Baalman, Olivier Landry-Gagnon, AVX Lab (BunBun) and Rodrigo Velasco (yecto).


Special thanks to Erandy Vergara and Aaron Pollard
